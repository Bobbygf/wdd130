History

Founded in 2016 in Kochchikade, Sri Lanka, Rapid Adventures emerged with a bold vision: to create adrenaline-filled journeys that allow thrill-seekers to experience the raw beauty and excitement of Sri Lanka’s natural landscapes. Starting with humble rafting trips through the lush rivers of Kitulgala, the company quickly expanded into a dynamic force in adventure tourism, offering white water rafting, canyoning, hiking, kayaking, paramotoring, and ATV experiences across the island. Driven by a deep respect for nature and a commitment to safety, Rapid Adventures built its reputation on expertly guided tours, eco-conscious practices, and affordable, personalized service. With its seamless digital booking system and strong online presence, the company earned repeated honors as Sri Lanka’s Most Popular Travel and Tourism Website at the BestWeb.lk. Awards, most recently in 2024.

Today, Rapid Adventures operates across seven states with a fleet of over 80 rafts and 50 certified guides. We’ve pioneered safety innovations now used industry-wide, including our patented rapid-assessment system and eco-friendly raft materials.

We're proud to have introduced over 250,000 adventurers to whitewater rafting while maintaining a perfect safety record and donating 1% of all profits to river conservation efforts.
